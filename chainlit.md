# Welcome!

---

## How this works

This system conducts a structured, research-based interview to identify practical AI opportunities inside your organization.

**What happens:**
1. You answer a short set of questions about yourself and your company.
2. The agent may look up your company online for context, and you can confirm or correct that summary before the interview continues.
3. It will ask about your daily work, the tools you use, pain points, bottlenecks, and what takes the most time.
4. If you mention an important internal tool, system, or term, the agent may ask you to explain it rather than guessing.
5. The system adapts questions to your role and uses company memory from previous interviews to reduce redundant questioning.
6. At the end, it proposes AI use cases and asks for your feedback on how useful and feasible they seem in practice.

---

## What we'll explore
- Your main tasks and responsibilities
- Where you spend the most time
- What feels repetitive, tedious, manual, or error-prone
- The tools, systems, and workflows involved
- Your goals and KPIs
- Your perspective on data, regulations, explainability, and technical blockers

---

## Important
- The more concrete your examples are, the better the results.
- You can skip questions or say "I don't know"
- If you see company info that is wrong, just say "no" and you'll be asked to describe your company.
- If a proposed AI use case belongs to another team or manager, you can say so.
- Your information is confidential and used for research and assessment only.
