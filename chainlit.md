# Welcome!

---

## How this works

This system conducts a structured, research-based interview to help you discover opportunities for AI automation, workflow improvement, and value creation in your organization.

**What happens:**
1. You answer a short set of questions about yourself and your company.
2. The agent may look up your company online for context (and you can correct it if needed).
3. It will ask about your daily work, pain points, and what slows you down.
4. The system adapts questions to your role and remembers what other colleagues from your company have already shared (no redundant questions!).
5. At the end, it generates a report with high-value AI opportunities tailored to your situation.

---

## What we'll explore
- Your main tasks and responsibilities
- Where you spend the most time
- What feels repetitive, tedious, manual, or error-prone
- Your goals and KPIs
- Your perspective on data, regulations, and technical blockers

---

## Important
- The more detail you give, the better the results!
- You can skip questions or say "I don't know"
- If you see company info that is wrong, just say "no" and you'll be asked to describe your company.
- Your information is confidential and used for research/assessment only.
